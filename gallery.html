this / is / maxi



Hey! My name is Maxime Perreault, but you can call me Maxi. I’m a queer québécois artist working with mixed-digital medias and I create using a few different tools, software, methods, and programming languages. I am also a tattoo artist working in Tiohtià:ke (Montreal).

Digital medias excite me. I get inspired a lot by new software functionalities, new algorithms, new methods, I guess you could say by novelty in general. Another thing would be the human body, or the deformation of it. It took me a while to understand why I loved body modifications so much, but I think the main reason is that I can have authority over my own physical form through them. As a tattoo artist, it brings me a lot of joy to know I can help others with negative ideas of their own bodies, sometimes taking shape as body dysmorphia, fat-shame, and internalized self-hate. As a digital artist, it makes me want to explore different ways to represent bodies and physical health, whether it's through deformed humanoid 3d models, data visualization of illnesses and addictions, or organic generative art.
