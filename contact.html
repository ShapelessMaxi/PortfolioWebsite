Artist Statement

Rooted in queer theory and influenced by my own personal experiences as a queer subject, my work speaks of identity, embodiment, and visibility. Through the synthesis of art, technology, and humanities, I strive to push the boundaries of conventional artistic expression. My love for tattooing allows me to help people define their identity through iconography. It is wildly validating to have people trust me with such permanent decisions and motivates me to be a better artist and person. Tattoos are a huge inspiration in my digital media pieces, as they are closely tied to some of the recurrent themes I love to explore.
